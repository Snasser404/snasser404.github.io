Drop marketing & design assets here (campaign screenshots, mockups, banners, creative).

Two ways to use them in src/data/content.ts:
  1) Case-study cards  -> set 'image' on an entry in marketingProjects, e.g. image: '/marketing/globaldws.png'
  2) Design gallery    -> set 'image' on an entry in designGallery, e.g. image: '/marketing/social-1.png'

Until you add images, the design gallery shows labelled 'add asset' placeholder tiles.
